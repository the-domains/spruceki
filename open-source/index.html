Cache Dup

My biggest bugbear with NoSQL in general is the problem of denormalisation, leading to lots of duplicate records which need to be "manually" kept in sync by the application. We’ve been working on a wrapper around Infinispan that tracks what goes in and automatically maintains referential integrity across denormalised data.
